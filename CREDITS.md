Coverage gaps in the free pool worth knowing about, for future owned photography:

- **Rothschild's giraffe at Lake Nakuru** — listed as a signature experience; no suitable free image, so it is not shown visually.
- **Tsavo's red-dust elephants** — the colouring that makes Tsavo distinctive barely appears in the free pool; the current shots read as generic bush.
- **Mount Kenya behind Laikipia** — the framing the card copy uses is not available free; the hero shows the plateau without the mountain.
- **Rhino tracking on foot / walking safaris** — no free images of guided walking exist; the Laikipia rhino shot is a vehicle-distance photo.

Treat this set as launch scaffolding, and replace hero images first as real material comes in.
